About the team :

Ksaipra Racing is a budding Formula SAE student team from VIT University, Vellore. We build cars - Formula SAE Racing mobiles. We are the worshippers of the Racing Cult and we engineer automobiles with pure devotion, let alone fanatic passion. Participation in the FSAE events held worldwide is our core competency besides developing future-ready tech.
